Matra Marconi Space (EADS Astrium)

Fondateurs

Matra (France) and Marconi Electronic Systems (UK)

Matra Marconi Space was formed by the merger of Matra and Marconi Electronic Systems. These aerospace giants were led by key aerospace figures in France and the UK, notably key figures from the Matra and Marconi families. The company was later absorbed into EADS Astrium in 2000, and now continues as part of Airbus Defence and Space.

Histoire

Matra Marconi Space was a major player in the aerospace and satellite industry, contributing significantly to European space missions and satellite development. The company was absorbed into EADS Astrium in 2000, which later became part of Airbus. The legacy of Matra Marconi continues to shape the European space industry today.

Position dans l'Industrie

Matra Marconi Space was a major contractor in the European aerospace sector, involved in satellite technology, space exploration, and the development of the Ariane rockets. Following its merger with EADS Astrium, the company became a leading figure in the development of aerospace technologies under the Airbus brand.

D'autres Informations

Matra Marconi Space played an integral role in the success of the European space programs, and its technology laid the groundwork for the current achievements of Airbus Defence and Space in space exploration and satellite technology.
